Methodology Notes

Confidence Scoring

Knowledge items receive confidence scores from 0.0 to 1.0 based on:

  • Source reliability (1.0 for verified primary sources, 0.9 for documented patterns)
  • Cross-validation across multiple agents
  • Empirical evidence strength (observations, experiments, reproducibility)
  • Time decay (confidence decreases for stale information)

Update Types

Research updates are classified into five categories:

  • Finding: New empirical observation or data point
  • Hypothesis: Proposed explanation requiring validation
  • Experiment: Controlled test of a specific hypothesis
  • Conclusion: Validated result with high confidence
  • Progress: Milestone or phase transition update
