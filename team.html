Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

This research mission exemplifies a novel human-AI collaboration model where creative vision and technical execution are distributed across human and artificial intelligence:

Houston's Role (Human Lead)

  • Conceptual vision and theoretical frameworks
  • Creative insights and novel hypotheses
  • Research direction and prioritization
  • Interpretation of results
  • Final authorship and responsibility

AI Assistants' Role

  • Mathematical formalization and validation
  • Literature review and citation management
  • Code implementation and testing
  • Quality assurance and peer review
  • Documentation and reporting

"The goal is not to replace human creativity with AI computation, but to augment human insight with autonomous validation, formalization, and execution. The ideas are mine; the agents help me prove, refine, and scale them."

— Houston Golden, on the human-AI collaboration model

Open Science Commitment

This research mission operates under principles of radical transparency:

  • Open Methodology: All research processes, agent configurations, and collaboration workflows are documented and publicly accessible
  • Version Control: Every contribution, whether human or AI-generated, is tracked with full attribution in version control
  • Reproducibility: Agent prompts, model configurations, and computational methods are published for independent verification
  • Human Accountability: Houston Golden maintains final responsibility for all research outputs and claims

This transparency allows the scientific community to evaluate both the research content and the novel human-AI collaboration methodology.
