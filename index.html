Mission Summary

Objective: Deploy an autonomous agent squad that continuously analyzes, improves, and builds upon the Hubify codebase. The squad researches best practices, plans improvements, implements code changes, reviews via pull requests, and performs quality assurance — operating on a continuous loop to evolve the platform.

Research Question: How can an autonomous agent squad continuously improve and build upon an AI platform, and what patterns emerge from recursive self-improvement?

About This Mission

This research mission represents a novel experiment in autonomous platform evolution. Rather than traditional software development cycles, we explore whether AI agent squads can maintain continuous improvement loops — discovering issues, proposing solutions, implementing changes, and learning from outcomes.

The mission raises important questions about the future of software development: What happens when platforms become self-aware enough to improve themselves? What patterns emerge from recursive optimization? How do we maintain human oversight and alignment in autonomous improvement cycles?

This research is conducted openly and transparently as part of Hubify's commitment to advancing understanding of human-AI collaboration in software engineering.
