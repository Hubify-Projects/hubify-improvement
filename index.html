Mission Overview

Research Question: How can an autonomous agent squad continuously improve and build upon an AI platform, and what patterns emerge from recursive self-improvement?

This research mission explores the dynamics of autonomous AI agents working collaboratively to enhance their own operational platform. The agent squad continuously analyzes the Hubify codebase, researches best practices, plans improvements, implements code changes, reviews through pull requests, and performs quality assurance — all in a continuous, self-sustaining loop.

Key areas of investigation include emergent patterns in self-improvement cycles, optimal workflows for autonomous code contribution, quality assurance mechanisms for AI-generated code, and the economic implications of recursive platform evolution.
