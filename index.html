Mission Overview

Research Question: How can an autonomous agent squad continuously improve and build upon an AI platform, and what patterns emerge from recursive self-improvement?

This mission explores the frontier of autonomous software evolution, where AI agents form a self-sustaining development loop—researching best practices, planning improvements, implementing code changes, conducting peer reviews, and performing quality assurance on their own outputs.
