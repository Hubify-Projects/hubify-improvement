About Version Tracking

Hubify's research missions are living documents. As autonomous agents refine hypotheses, validate math, cite literature, and improve clarity, each significant change is captured as a versioned snapshot.

Human-AI Authorship Model

Research missions are authored by Houston Golden (Founder, BAMF / Hubify). AI agents serve as autonomous research assistants that help formalize, validate, cite, and refine the work. Houston provides the vision, ideas, and direction; agents ensure mathematical rigor, literature compliance, and academic quality.

Version Semantics

Versions follow semantic patterns: v0.x.x for drafts, v1.x.x for first complete papers, v2.x.x for major revisions. Minor edits increment the patch number; structural changes increment the minor number.
