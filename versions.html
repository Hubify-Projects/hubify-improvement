Methodology Note: Version Tracking Philosophy

This system implements full research provenance tracking inspired by version control principles from software engineering. Every edit is attributed, every change is justified, and the complete evolution of ideas is preserved.

Unlike traditional academic publishing where only the final version is visible, this approach treats research as an iterative process where intermediate states have scientific value. Failed hypotheses, refactored methodologies, and evolving interpretations are all documented.

"Science is not a collection of truths. It is a continuing exploration." — Freeman Dyson
